Launching Byteworks

Posted on February 2nd, 2025 by Rohan Mishra

We officially launched Byteworks this week with our first meeting. The focus was on introducing the club's goals and gathering project ideas from members. A few of us discussed building AI tools that could help students study more efficiently. We’ve also started organizing our roles and project documentation using shared drives. It’s exciting to see this come together from scratch.

AI Tools Brainstorm

Posted on February 9th, 2025 by Aryaveer Dhawan

This week’s meeting focused on brainstorming potential AI projects. Members proposed an NFL game predictor, a Chrome extension to limit short-form video distractions, and a tennis court locator. We also explored how LLMs like ChatGPT could be used in creative or practical student tools. The group discussions were energetic and productive. Everyone is now forming teams around shared interests.

Project Teams Finalized

Progress tracking will begin next week.
